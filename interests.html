Stock Market Investing

Another one of my passions is stock market investing. I have been an active investor since the age of 18. I have had great gains in the thousands as well as horrible losses in the thousands. My two favorite mentors in investing are Warren Buffet and Charlie Munger. I have read the autobiography of Warren Buffet and recommend it to anyone trying to learn about stock market investments. It is easy to read and very interesting. I find their advice refreshing and practical in a world where social media “gurus” try to convince everyone that buying their course on trading options will make them a millionaire.
